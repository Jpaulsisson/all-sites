WHO AM I?

I am no one. Just a hairless monkey, with snakes crawling around in his head. If I have a silver-lining it's certainly my persistence in learning. When you know you're dumb, you do a lot more things to make yourself un-dumb. Being from a small town in Alabama, the world had made up their mind that I drove a lawn mower to a school of 30 kids, until I could get my dad's hand-me-down pickup truck and go to work at the auto-shop. None of those stereotypes ever fit me much. Though I did grow up in the woods, with very few neighbors and rode dirtbikes with friends for most of my childhood. I have chewed tobacco too...hmm maybe I am a hick. I started traveling pretty seriously when I hit my 20s and have seen a ton of my bucketlist already. That includes a 6 month stay in London, where I earned my professional certification as a master barber. In the end, I just like to learn and now that I've discovered coding, it's like there's a bottomless well of knowledge in front of me.

WHAT AM I DOING HERE?

Well, I'm essentially creating a website that will serve as a time capsule/journal on my road to becoming a full-stack developer. Coding has awakened a desire for learning and growing in me that I haven't felt in a while. It is very challenging but when you click run and debug and see your own algorithm run perfectly, what a feeling. Chills. A rush of satisfaction and accomplishment. All those hours googling "how do I...in CSS" and "how to...in JS", they've finally paid off. You made it work! It's truly a beautiful experience. So, what am I doing here? Hoping to remind my future-self and you, to just keep going and find the fire in you. You got this.
